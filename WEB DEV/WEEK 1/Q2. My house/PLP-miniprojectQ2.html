MY HOUSE

A home is a place where a person feels protected from the tension of everyday life. In
My house, I always feel safe and secure and I always want to spend as much time as possible
there. I live in a very beautiful house. It is located deep in the large city of Lusaka
the capital city of Zambia.

How My House Is Built

My house is built in quite a busy area. There's a bus stop just two blocks away
a secondary school and a University just within a kilometer radius. Despite being this busy,
the are is a quiet place. My house is a three roomed house with a big livingroom ,a Kitchen and
a beautiful bedroom. It is self contained. It is a small house and I like it that way.
